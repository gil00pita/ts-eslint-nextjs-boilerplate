<Callout title="Rules" tone="tip">
  - Motion should reveal relationship, not attract attention for its own sake. - Non-essential
  motion must respect reduced-motion preferences. - Loading motion should communicate activity
  without suggesting precise progress. - Avoid movement that shifts layout after people start
  reading.
</Callout>
