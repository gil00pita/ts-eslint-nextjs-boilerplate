<Callout title="Visual Direction" tone="tip">
  - Simple geometric forms with clear silhouettes. - Limited color, usually neutral surfaces with
  one Ember or Sky accent. - Human figures only when they clarify scale or collaboration. - No
  decorative background blobs or abstract atmosphere.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Production Requirements" tone="warning">
  - Provide descriptive alt text when the image adds meaning. - Use empty alt text when the image is
  decorative. - Keep file sizes small and export at the required display size. - Test illustrations
  in light and dark themes.
</Callout>
